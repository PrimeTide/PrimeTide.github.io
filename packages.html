Three ways to work together

Packages that build from a first steady system to a fully tuned workshop.

Each tier is built on the one before it. Starter stabilizes the essentials. Core System turns your tools into a dependable daily rhythm. Atelier adds space for deeper experimentation and ongoing refinement.

Engagements are quoted based on your tools, volume, and market. These outlines show scope and shape, not rigid bundles.

Comparison

Choose the level of system you want to live in

All packages respect your existing stack. No platform migrations, no surprise add-ons. Each step up simply carries more of the work for you.

Starter
A first steady system for solo agents or very small teams.
A focused engagement that repairs the biggest leaks in intake and follow-up using the tools already in place.
Includes
  • Mapping of existing lead sources, basic workflows, and key bottlenecks.
  • Connection of 1–2 primary lead sources into your CRM (website, portal, or form).
  • Warm, on-brand first response templates reviewed and approved before launch.
  • Simple tagging structure for source and basic intent so leads are never “just in there.”
  • Daily or weekly summary of new leads so nothing disappears into the inbox.

Best when the current pain is “leads arrive, but the follow-up feels scattered or late.”

Most common
Core System
For agents and teams who want intake, follow-up, and operations aligned.
Builds on Starter and turns your CRM into a true command center instead of a crowded list.
Everything in Starter, plus

Starter foundations are refined, then extended across more of your day-to-day work.

  • Additional lead sources connected (website forms, portals, open house signups, referrals).
  • Structured pipelines and stages that match how you actually sell, not generic defaults.
  • Light nurture sequences for “not yet” buyers and sellers, tuned to your tone and pacing.
  • Calendar alignment for consult calls and key follow-up blocks.
  • Ongoing adjustment window to tweak timing, wording, and routing as real leads move through.

A fit when the goal is one calm, dependable system that supports a full book of business.

Atelier Package
For agents treating their practice as a long-term, evolving workshop.
Builds on Core System with space for deeper experimentation, new flows, and regular collaborative refinement.
Everything in Core System, plus

The system grows with you: new ideas can be tested without breaking what already works.

  • Additional custom flows, such as seller intake, post-close care, and referral tracking.
  • Deeper reporting on bottlenecks, response patterns, and where leads quietly stall.
  • Regular strategy sessions to decide what to adjust, simplify, or extend next.
  • Room for thoughtful experiments with new tools or channels, without starting from scratch.
  • Priority scheduling for changes when your market or volume shifts quickly.

Chosen by agents who want a long-term partner in the background, not just a one-time build.

How scope and pricing are set

Before any work begins, we map your tools, volume, and goals. From there, you receive a clear written outline of the proposed build, the package it belongs in, and the investment for that scope — no hidden tiers or surprise add-ons.

What stays the same in every package
  • Your existing tools remain the foundation wherever possible.
  • Language and timing are reviewed with you before going live.
  • Talos is tuned to support your relationships, not replace them.
  • You always know what the system is doing and why.
