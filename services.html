What We Automate

We design and build intelligent workflows that connect your forms, calendar, email, CRM, and messaging tools—so your business runs like a polished machine.

Lead Capture & First Response

  • Capture leads from websites, portals, and ads into one place.
  • Instantly send a personalized text or email when a new inquiry arrives.
  • Tag and route leads based on source, location, or criteria.

Follow-Up & Nurture

  • Timed follow-up sequences when someone goes quiet.
  • Reminder tasks and notifications when it’s time to call.
  • Lightweight nurture campaigns for old leads that might re-activate.

CRM & Data Hygiene

  • Automatic creation and updating of contacts and deals in your CRM.
  • De-duplication rules so you don’t get five versions of the same person.
  • Syncing key fields between forms, spreadsheets, and your CRM.

Admin & Operations Automation

  • Auto-generated tasks and checklists when a deal hits a new stage.
  • Internal notifications to your team when certain conditions are met.
  • Simple dashboards or reports so you always know what needs attention.
