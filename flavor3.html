Example intake flow for a new website lead

Step 01
Lead submits your existing form
Talos listens behind the scenes—no change to your website, no additional embed required.
Time: 0–2 seconds
Step 02
Instant reply from your brand
A warm, on-brand message acknowledges the inquiry, sets expectations, and asks one or two targeted questions.
Time: 2–15 seconds
Step 03
Talos captures key details
Budget, timing, location, and whether they’re already working with an agent—all recorded in the background.
Time: 1–3 minutes (as they reply)
Step 04
Your CRM is updated
A contact record is created or updated with tags, notes, and tasks that match your pipeline stages.
Time: instant after Talos has the data

You receive a concise summary
Instead of raw form data, you see a short narrative of who they are, what they need, and what Talos recommends you do next.
Time: within minutes
